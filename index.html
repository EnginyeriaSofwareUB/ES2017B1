Welcome to SlyHunting

The newest release on turn-based strategy games.

In this game you gonna we completely immerse in a 2D world based on the deep and crazy life between rivers, rocks, trees and mountains: the jungle.

It also has to be said, this is not a normal jungle where the way of nature runs predictably and in the expected order of things, here the empowerment of the inhabitants of the jungle has rise up to the top.

We are living in an open war for survival, that have two clear factions . I one hand, we have the hunters, extremely anxious in the collection of death bodies, native from the jungle, to sell it in the black market. They have spent years exterminating a dozen of different species, and now is the turn of monkeys. This is the other hand, and the unexpected one.

Is natural to thing thats this war is gonna be we unfair and unbalanced, but this idea don't fit exactly with the reality. The things are becoming a bit weird. It turns out that the past of the years and the destruction caused by the hunters, has inspired to monkeys to build the best method of self-defense, still the arms of the humans and learn to use it. An eye for an eye.

You gonna choose in which side of the combat you are with. But remember one thing, only the team based strategy and the attention to the environment are the keys to becoming the winner team.

Main Menu

This is the main menu screen where three buttons appear so you can continue browsing the game, one of them is the play to start the game, another is the configuration of the game where you can choose if you want to activate all the sounds of the animations or of the background and even modify the settings of the screen and another button to exit the game.

Select Players

This is the selection screen. Here you have to make a firm decision about which team you take part in this war. Remember that in this reality animals are intelligent like humans, even more because they have a great spirit of revenge. You can differentiate the number of player per team you will control. Be crazy an put the maximum and you will spend a long and destructive game.

Our Team
